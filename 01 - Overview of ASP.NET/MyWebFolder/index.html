 How To Find Us
  If you're coming from outside the area, take the M4 to Junction 42, and follow signs for Swansea (about 4 miles to Swansea).

When you get to Swansea, you'll cross a river bridge. Stay in the nearside lane and follow signs for Gower/ Mumbles. Carry along the coast for 3 miles.

At the traffic island, turn right, then take the 1st right and the 2nd left.
